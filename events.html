Instructions for Uploading Photos

  1. Scan the QR code corresponding to the drive you want to upload to.
  2. A prompt will appear asking for a password.
  3. Enter the password provided alongside the QR code.
  4. If you enter the correct password, you will be redirected to the upload page.
  5. Upload your photos as instructed on the upload page.
  6. After completing your upload, you can return to this page.
  7. Please note: All uploads will be deleted 3 days after the event.
